FAQ

What is Store Agent?

Store Agent is an AI-powered field action platform for retail operations. It unifies store performance, environmental data, staffing schedules, and promotions into a single conversational interface — so every operator knows exactly what to do next, faster and with greater confidence.

How is it different from a regular dashboard?

A standard dashboard shows you numbers. Store Agent tells you what to do with them. Every data point comes paired with a concrete next action — generated by AI trained on accumulated store operations data and grounded in real outcomes from your own network.

What data sources does it integrate with?

Store Agent integrates with POS data, foot traffic analytics, weather APIs, staffing schedule systems, and promotion calendars. All data is processed within the customer's own infrastructure under strict access controls.

Can it be configured for our scale and environment?

Yes. Store Agent is designed to flex around your store structure, data systems, and operational workflows. Reach out to our sales team to scope the right implementation for your organization.
