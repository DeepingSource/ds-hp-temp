Common on-site problems

Out-of-stock

An empty shelf is lost revenue

The better a product sells, the more revenue slips away the moment its shelf goes empty. We watch shelf status continuously and flag out-of-stock signals quickly, so you never miss the moment to restock.

Inconsistency

Many stores, run as one

Even within one brand, display, cleanliness, and service vary by store. We compare each store by the same standard, so the best stores set the bar for every store.

Anomaly detection

Catch the moments easily missed

Crowding, falls, abnormal movement — situations hard for people to watch every time, watched for you. When an anomaly appears, it is sent to the right person at once for a fast response.
