Operating challenges of large spaces

Even a vast space, one flow

Crowd management

See the flow of a vast space at a glance

The larger and more multi-level a space, the harder it is to tell where it’s crowded. Watch zone-by-zone crowd flow on one screen, and prepare staffing and guidance in advance.

Flow analysis

Bring visitor flow into space operations

We watch the flow from entrance to stores to amenities. Identify where people linger and where they don’t — a basis for refining layout and guidance routes.

Anomaly detection

Anomaly detection with fewer blind spots

The larger the space, the harder for people to watch everywhere. We watch signals like overcrowding, falls, and abnormal situations, and send them to the right person at once for a fast response.

* Example analysis screen

Spatial AI · MTMC

Many cameras, as one space

MTMC (Multi-Target Multi-Camera) Spatial AI stitches the views of scattered cameras into a single space. It follows flow seamlessly across wide floors and levels, so a large space can be watched like one store.

Explore Spatial AI technology
  • Unify multiple camera views into one spatial coordinate system
  • Track continuous flow across floors and zones
  • Compare zone-by-zone crowding and flow by the same standard
