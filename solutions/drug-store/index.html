On-site problems in drugstores

Display

Display consistency in every store

With many categories and fast turnover, displays slip out of order easily. We watch shelf display by the same standard, so every store keeps a clean, orderly look.

Out-of-stock

Catch the gaps in popular items first

The more in-demand an item, the faster an empty shelf turns into a walk-away. We flag out-of-stock signals quickly, so you check the shelves to restock first.

Customer flow

Bring customer flow into operations

We watch the flow through areas where people linger and areas they pass by — a basis for reviewing display and layout and refining the store experience.
