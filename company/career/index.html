How We Work

How we work

“세상 모든 데이터를 안전하게, 세상 모든 공간을 완벽하게” — our vision and privacy principle carry through to everyday decisions.

Start from the problem

We start from real on-site problems, not technology. A single store owner’s question was our starting point.

Technology that keeps its principles

개인정보를 건드리지 않고 공간을 이해합니다. We work on an uncompromising privacy principle.

Prove it through execution

We don’t stop at analysis — we prove it through execution and results. The learning loop is how we work.

One team, like one store

We share the same goal without drawing boundaries. Every store, like one — and our team moves like one team, too.
