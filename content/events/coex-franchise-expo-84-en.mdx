## From opening to running, in one flow

From choosing where to open to managing and promoting once you're open — **Anonymized Spatial AI (SAAI)** helps with safe data. Come see it at COEX Hall D, August 25–27.

## What you'll see at the booth

- **Trade-area analysis — saai count · saai insight**: "Is this the right spot?" We count the footfall passing your storefront alongside the customers who walk in, reading the location by inflow rate against footfall — plus gender, age band, and hourly traffic.
- **Store management — saai care**: "I can't watch it all the time — is that okay?" It watches empty shelves, floor spills, fridge temperature, and anomalies for you, and flags only the moments that need you.
- **Product promotion — POP maker**: "How do I make it stand out?" Drop in a product image and a line of copy, and a poster or shelf sign is ready in a minute.

## On-site program

- **5-minute install demo**: Try the setup yourself — just an AI box and a phone.
- **1-minute POP**: Add a product, pick the copy and mood, and it's done on the spot.
- **1:1 startup consultation**: Book a session to shape a setup that fits your location and format.
