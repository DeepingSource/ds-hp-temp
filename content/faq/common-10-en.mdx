It isn't. Footage is anonymized in memory on the spot, and the original never lands anywhere in the system.

What remains is personal-data-free statistics — coordinates, gender band, age band, gaze, and pickup. No human eye ever reviews the footage, and identities cannot be recovered from the anonymized data.
