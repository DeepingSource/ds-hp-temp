No. Footage is anonymized in memory at the moment of input, and what remains is statistics that cannot identify any individual.

- Korea PIPA **Article 58-2 (anonymized data)** — anonymized information falls outside the scope of the Act.
- Confirmed by a **PIPC interpretation (Aug 2021)** and a **legal opinion (Dec 2021)**.
- GDPR (Recital 26) and the CCPA exclude anonymous information on the same principle.
