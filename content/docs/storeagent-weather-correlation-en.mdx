This feature lets you check the correlation between weather and sales, and factor external context into decisions such as ordering. Ask something like "What sells well on rainy days?" and it ranks the products most sensitive to weather. Use it to factor in forecasts, such as expected rain, before you place an order.

## Key Features

- **Weather-sensitive products** — Ranks products whose sales rise in specific weather.
- **Correlation evidence** — Shows the size of the change in sales versus normal, backed by data.
- **Apply to ordering** — Uses the forecast to help decide which items to stock first.

## How to Use

1. Ask in the chat window, for example: "What sells well on rainy days?"
2. Review the ranking of weather-sensitive products and the key summary.
3. Under "Details", check the size of the sales change for each product.
4. Factor it into your ordering plan, or register it as a note.

## Tips

- Check the forecast ahead of time and prepare your ordering and merchandising a day early to reduce stockouts.
- The ranking is a reference indicator, so weigh it alongside your store's inventory situation.
