This feature lets you ask about external trends such as new products and collaborations in natural language, then use the results to inform ordering and merchandising decisions. Ask something like "What are the hot new convenience store items this week?" and it summarizes recently launched products and trending items. Use it to set display priorities for new arrivals.

## Key Features

- **New product lookup** — Gathers recently launched products and collaboration items in one place.
- **Buzz summary** — Summarizes which products are drawing attention, along with the reasoning.
- **Merchandising reference** — Helps set display priorities by section, such as desserts and drinks.

## How to Use

1. Ask in the chat window, for example: "What are the hot new convenience store items this week?"
2. When the lookup finishes, review the key summary and product list.
3. Open "Details" to check supporting information such as product names and prices.
4. Register anything worth keeping as a Task or a note.

## Tips

- New products may have limited sales data, so watch how they actually perform after you display them.
- Naming a specific section (desserts, drinks, and so on) narrows the results.
