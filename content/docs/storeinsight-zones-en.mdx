## What it does

- **Passage zones** are areas along store paths used to analyze visitors' visits, stays, and pickup behavior.
- **No-Passage zones** target spaces that can't be walked through — such as shelves — and let you analyze visitor interest and behavior.

## Zone types at a glance

| Category | Zone type | Description | Reports it powers |
| ---- | --------- | ---- | ----------- |
| Passage | **General** | Divide the store into broad units to capture major movement trends and traffic | Zone analysis / Main path / Inter-Zone Traffic |
| Passage | **Interest** | Measure stays in front of shelves to understand interest and dwell patterns | Zone interest analysis / Visitor path / Heat map / Customer Touchpoint / Funnel |
| Passage | **Counter** | Determine purchase conversion based on extended dwelling at the counter | Purchase conversion analysis |
| Passage | **Staff** | Zones only staff can access — used to exclude staff from analysis | Most reports excluding Line analysis |
| Passage | **Dining** | Measure how seats are used to analyze occupancy | Seat Occupancy Rate |
| No-Passage | **Display stand** | Use non-walkable shelves as the basis for analyzing product interest and merchandising effectiveness | Funnel Analysis / Display Shelf Analysis / Display Shelf Heatmap |

## Zone Config screen (common)

### 1. Open the Zone Config screen

![Settings button at the bottom-left of the screen](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/01-settings-button.png)

*① Click the Settings button at the bottom-left of the screen*

Click the **Settings button** at the bottom-left of the screen to go to the Settings page.

![Zone Config menu in the Settings page](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/02-zones-menu.png)

*② Click Zone Config in the left-hand menu*

In the left-hand menu of the Settings page, click the **Zone Config** button to open the Zone Config screen.

### 2. Zone Config screen layout

![Main Zone Config screen](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/03-zones-screen.png)

*Passage/No-Passage selector, operating filter, drawing tools, search, and Save button*

- Pick the **Passage / No-Passage category** based on the report you want to power.
- Use the **In operation / Non-operation filter** to show only zones in a given operational state.
- Use the **drawing tools** to add or edit zones.
- Use the **search box** to find zones by name or tag keyword.
- Click the **Save button** to confirm zones.

#### Drawing tool reference

![Draw to Add tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/04-tool-draw.png)

*Draw to Add — connect points to add a zone*

![Add Rectangle tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/05-tool-rect.png)

*Add Rectangle — add a precise rectangular zone*

![Duplicate tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/06-tool-duplicate.png)

*Duplicate — clone the selected zone identically*

![Redraw tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/07-tool-redraw.png)

*Redraw — reset the selected zone*

![Overlay Layer tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/08-tool-layer.png)

### 3. Confirm the zone

> **💡 팁**
>
> After you draw a zone, you can review it in the minimap below.

![Zone confirmation screen — Order, entrance checkbox, color/name, zone type, tags, operating period](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/09-confirm-zone.png)

*Set the zone's metadata and operating period, then save*

- Edit the **Order** field to change the order in which data is displayed.
- For zones where customers first enter, tick the **entrance checkbox** to designate them as entrance zones.
  - Once an entrance zone is set, the Main Path Report becomes available.
  - You can designate multiple entrance zones.
- Set the **zone's color and name**.
- To change or add zone types, **select or deselect zone types**.
- After picking the zone type, you can **add detailed attributes as tags (keywords)** to make zones easier to search and manage.
  - Tags not assigned to any zone are deleted automatically.
  - This field is optional, so you can leave it blank.
- Set the **zone's operating period**.
  - For new zones, the start date defaults to `today` and the end date to `2099.12.31`.
  - Zones whose operating period has ended move to the Non-operation tab.
- To delete a zone you've drawn, click the **X button**.

## Setting up passage zones

### General zones

> Reports it powers: `Zone analysis`, `Main path`, `Inter-Zone Traffic`

![General zone example — store divided into broad units](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/10-general-zone.png)

*General zones drawn as broad, non-overlapping units across the store*

General zones aren't meant for detailed shelf-level analysis — they capture customers' **larger movement (Flow)** across the store.

- Divide the store into **broad, non-overlapping units**.
- Splitting it into narrow units makes overall traffic harder to read and reduces the meaning of dwell time.

### Interest zones

> Reports it powers: `Zone interest analysis`, `Visitor path`, `Heat map`, `Customer Touchpoint`, `Funnel Analysis`

![Interest zone example — area in front of a shelf](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/11-interest-zone.png)

*An area close to the shelf, used to measure stays, gaze, and pickup*

Interest zones are designed to analyze the moments when customers **actually face a product or shelf**.

- Designate the area immediately in front of a shelf so stay, gaze, and pickup data are recorded.
- Draw the **zone right in front of the shelf** where visitors examine items in detail as a single interest zone.

### Counter zones

> Reports it powers: `Purchase conversion analysis`

![Counter zone example](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/12-checkout-zone.png)

*Drawn at the counter area — extended dwelling is treated as a purchase*

- Determine purchase based on dwelling in the walkable area in front of the counter.
- These are typically drawn at the **counter area**; dwelling beyond a threshold counts as a purchase.

### Staff zones

> Reports it powers: `Line analysis`, `Zone analysis`, `Visitor path`, `Main path`, `Inter-Zone Traffic`, `Heat map`, `Heatmap by time`, `Zone interest analysis`

![Staff zone example](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/13-staff-zone.png)

*Areas only staff can access — anyone passing through is identified as staff*

- Use this zone type to determine whether a person detected by CCTV is a staff member.
- Draw it where **only staff can access**; anyone with a record of passing through is treated as staff.

### Dining zones

> Reports it powers: `Seat Occupancy Rate`

![Dining zone example](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/14-seating-zone.png)

*An area where seating use occurs*

- Use this zone type to see how heavily the seats inside the zone are actually used.
- Draw zones where visitors actually use seats as dining zones.

## Setting up no-passage zones

### Display stand zones

> Reports it powers: `Funnel Analysis`, `Display Shelf Analysis`, `Display Shelf Heatmap`

![Display stand zone example](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/zones/15-display-zone.png)

*No-Passage zones drawn to match shelf positions*

- After updating the shelf floor information, click the **Update shelf floor information button**.
- This zone type analyzes behavior around non-walkable shelves.
- Draw zones to **match where shelves are placed**.
- **Set display stand zone names to match the corresponding interest zone names.**
