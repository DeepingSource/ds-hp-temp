It shows how crowded each zone of the store is right now, in real time. Ask by zone name and it reports the current congestion level and a staffing recommendation, and you can review the camera feed alongside.

## Key Features

- **Congestion by zone** — Reports the current number of customers and the congestion level of a specific zone in real time.
- **Key-metric cards** — Organizes current customers, staff, average dwell time, and the week-over-week change on one screen.
- **LIVE camera feed** — Shows on-site views from multiple channels together, refreshed at short intervals.

## How to Use

1. Ask about the zone name you are curious about in the chat box.
2. The real-time congestion analysis step checks the current status.
3. Check the congestion level and the staffing recommendation in the key summary.
4. Review the site directly with the metric cards on the right and the LIVE feed.

## Tips

- Congestion can change even over a short time, so watch a little longer before adjusting staff.
- Use the week-over-week figure to gauge whether it is busier or quieter than usual right now.
