The store overview is a dashboard that always shows your store metrics on the right side of the screen. You can see four customer metrics and four store metrics at a glance, and change the query basis with the period filter at the top. Below that come new products and news, followed by recommended actions and manager memos.

## Key features

- **Four customer metrics** — daily visitor count, male-to-female visit ratio, visit ratio by age, and conversion rate.
- **Four store metrics** — daily out-of-stock trend, daily cleaning score, basket analysis, and shelf conversion rate.
- **Period filter** — switch the metrics by weekday, weekend, last two weeks, or previous month.
- **Age groups** — the visit ratio by age is split into under 30s, 30s, 40s, 50s, and 60s and over.

## How to use it

1. Choose one of weekday, weekend, last two weeks, or previous month from the period filter at the top.
2. Check the flow of visitor count, gender, age, and conversion rate in the customer metrics area.
3. Review out-of-stock, cleaning, basket, and shelf conversion rate in the store metrics area.
4. When a metric catches your eye, follow up with a related question in the chat on the left.

## Tips

- Alternating between the weekday and weekend filters on the same metric reveals differences in your operating patterns.
- The higher the multiplier value in the basket analysis, the stronger the combination of items sold together.
