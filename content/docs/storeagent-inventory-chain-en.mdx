It flags in advance the chain in which low inventory leads to stockouts, and stockouts in turn spread into lost sales. Ask whether there are items at risk of running out, and it reports the results of a check based on risk level.

## Key Features

- **Stockout-risk check** — Checks whether any items exceed the risk-level threshold and summarizes them as a number of alerts.
- **Term clarification** — For expressions with multiple meanings, such as a grade, it guides you to reconfirm based on the exact product name.
- **Proactive-check guidance** — Even below alert level, it flags fast-selling items so you can check their inventory in advance.

## How to Use

1. Ask about the stockout situation you are worried about in the chat box.
2. The risk-check step scans items by risk level.
3. Check the number of risk alerts in the key summary.
4. Review the basis for the judgment and the risk signals that may follow under "Details."

## Tips

- Rather than broad expressions such as a grade or range, asking by product name returns more accurate results.
- Even when there are no risk alerts, check the inventory of fast-turnover items periodically.
