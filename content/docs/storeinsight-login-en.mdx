StoreInsight is accessed through a web browser. Log in with the user ID and password issued by your administrator.

![StoreInsight login screen](/images/docs/storeinsight/en/getting-started/_images/login/login-screen.png)

*Login screen*

## Access URL

The access URL differs depending on the store you operate. Enter the URL you were issued into your web browser's address bar.

## Login steps

1. Enter your user ID in the **user ID** field.
2. Enter your password in the **Password** field.
3. Click the **Login** button to go to the dashboard.

> **💡 Tip: Trouble logging in?**
>
> If checking your user ID and password, the Caps Lock state, or clearing your browser cache doesn't resolve the issue, see the "If you can't log in" section of the [FAQ](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-faq).
