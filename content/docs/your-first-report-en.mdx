A guide to the process from installation being complete, through data accumulating, to reviewing your first report. How to read the full dashboard report in detail is covered in each product's user manual.

## The Flow After Installation

Once the installation and account-provisioning stages of deployment are complete, data collection based on your store's CCTV footage starts automatically, with no separate action required. It takes some time for the collected data to be organized into a report, and your account manager will advise you on the exact timing during dashboard training.

## Data Collection

Once installation is complete, visit and path data generated during store operating hours continues to accumulate in an anonymized state. Because reports are calculated based on this data, it is most accurate to review them after a certain amount of data has accumulated over business days. To see the characteristics of a specific day of the week or time slot, you may need that many more days of data.

## How to Review a Report

1. Go to the access address you were issued and log in with your ID and password.
2. Select "Store Status" from the report menu on the left — you can check the most basic metrics first, such as visitor count and dwell time.
3. Use the date-range selector at the top to specify the range of dates for which data has accumulated. If you have just installed, start by reviewing a short period first.

## What to Look At

At first, we recommend checking the following four items first. The full report structure and the detailed meaning of each metric are covered in the store insight user manual.

- **Visitor analysis** — Check the store's basic vitality through visitor count, estimated gender/age, and dwell time.
- **Line analysis** — Based on a line set at the entrance or elsewhere, check the number of passersby and visitors and the capture rate.
- **Zone analysis** — Compare visitor inflow and dwell time by shelf and corner to see which spaces get a good response.
- **Heatmap** — Check the areas where movement and dwell concentrate by color to grasp the overall flow of the store at a glance.

## If the Data Looks Empty

1. Check whether the date-range selector at the top actually overlaps with the dates on which data has accumulated.
2. If any filters such as day of the week or time slot are applied, clear them and check again.
3. If it is still empty, there may be a problem with the installation or integration status, so contact your account manager.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When can I review my first report?
You can review it as data accumulates on business days after installation is complete. You can be advised of the exact timing during dashboard training.

### A specific period looks strangely empty.
It may have been a closed day, or there may have been a temporary problem with the camera or network. If it recurs, contact your account manager.

### Can I view data from before installation?
No. Reports only cover data from the point at which anonymized analysis began.
