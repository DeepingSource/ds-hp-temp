## What it does

- Draw a **virtual line** wherever you want inside the store, and the system automatically counts and analyzes the customers who cross it.
- Combined with gender and age estimation and zone-by-zone inflow comparison, you can capture customer behavior in detail.

## 1. Open the Line Config screen

![Settings button at the bottom-left of the screen](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/01-settings-button.png)

*① Click the Settings button at the bottom-left of the screen*

Click the **Settings button** at the bottom-left of the screen to go to the Settings page.

![Line Config menu in the Settings page](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/02-lines-menu.png)

*② Click Line Config in the left-hand menu*

In the left-hand menu of the Settings page, click the **Line Config** button to open the Line Config screen.

## 2. Create a line

![+ Add Line button](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/03-add-line.png)

*The + Add Line button at the bottom of the Line Config screen*

Click the **[+ Add Line] button** at the bottom of the Line Config screen to create a new line.

## 3. Draw the line

![Active line with handles](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/04-line-handles.png)

*The active line is highlighted in blue, with square handles at each end for adjusting position, length, and angle*

Drag the line to the location you want to analyze and **adjust its length and angle** to draw the line.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> - The active line is highlighted in blue, and square handles appear at both ends.
> - Use the square handles at each end to adjust position, length, and angle.

> **⚠️ Caution: Line direction**
>
> Adjust the central arrow (◀) on the line to match the direction in which customers enter.
> (If customers enter in the ← direction, point the central arrow ◀.)

> **📌 Note**
>
> If you also need to analyze customers leaving the store, contact the product team to update your configuration.

## 4. Confirm the line

![Line name input and entrance checkbox](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/05-confirm-line.png)

*Enter a line name and mark it as an entrance if needed*

- Once the line position is final, **enter a line name**.
- To designate the line as an entrance, **tick the checkbox**.
- To delete a line you've drawn, click the **X button**.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> Reordering rows in the second column (Order) of the table changes the order in which data is displayed.

![Save button at the top right](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/lines/06-save-button.png)

*Click Save to confirm the line settings*

Click the **Save button** at the top right to confirm the lines.

> **⚠️ Caution**
>
> Saving requires **at least one** line marked as an entrance.
