This feature lets you search store operations know-how such as merchandising, ordering, and promotions in natural language. Ask something like "How should I handle a rainy day? Give me ordering and merchandising tips" and it summarizes operational advice suited to the situation. You can rely on it even when you haven't built up that experience yet.

## Key Features

- **Situational know-how** — Suggests operational tips suited to conditions such as weather, day of the week, and time of day.
- **Ordering and merchandising advice** — Suggests which items to stock and where to place them.
- **Check the reasoning** — Revisit the background behind each piece of advice, with data, under "Details".

## How to Use

1. Type the operational situation you're curious about into the chat window, in natural language.
2. Review the recommended direction in the key summary.
3. Open "Details" to see specifics such as items and placement.
4. Register any items you plan to act on as a Task or a note.

## Tips

- The more specific the situation you describe (for example, a rainy evening), the more tailored the advice.
- Recommendations are for reference only; make the final call based on your own store's situation.
