Explains what data is retained and for how long, and how to request deletion. The detailed retention period for each contract follows the privacy policy and the contract, and the exact details can be confirmed through your account manager.

## What Is Retained

The data DeepingSource handles falls broadly into two types. For the detailed principle of anonymization, refer to the How Anonymization Works document.

### Original CCTV footage
It passes through only briefly during the anonymization process and **is not stored in the DeepingSource system.**

### Anonymized behavioral data
Data organized in a form that cannot identify individuals, such as visits, paths, and dwell time, retained for the purpose of providing reports.

## How Long It Is Retained

Anonymized data is retained for as long as needed to provide period-comparison reports (month-over-month, year-over-year, etc.). The specific retention period may vary depending on the contract terms and the privacy policy, so please check the contract documents or contact your account manager for the exact criteria.

## Rights of the Data Subject

Under relevant laws such as the Personal Information Protection Act and GDPR, data subjects have the right to request access to, correction or deletion of, and suspension of the processing of information related to them. However, because the data DeepingSource retains is anonymized and in a form that cannot identify a specific individual, it may be structurally difficult to single out an individual visitor to look up or delete their information. Please submit related inquiries to the contact below.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What happens to the data when the contract ends?
Data handling upon contract termination (deletion, return, etc.) follows the conditions specified in the contract. Please contact your account manager for the exact procedure.

### Can the retention period be adjusted to fit our company's policy?
This is something that can be negotiated depending on the contract terms. Please let us know your requirements during the deployment discussion.

### Is anonymized data also treated as personal data?
It is processed in a form that cannot identify individuals, but the exact legal classification may vary depending on the governing laws and the method of processing. For details, refer to the privacy policy.
