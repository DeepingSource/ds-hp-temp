The date picker lets you specify the query period for a report. Beyond simple date selection, it also supports comparison analysis against another period.

## 1. Open the date picker

![Date button on the top navigation bar](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/01-open-button.png)

*① Click the date button at the bottom-right of the top navigation bar*

Clicking the **Date** button at the bottom-right of the top navigation bar opens the date picker.

![Date picker dropdown](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/02-dropdown.png)

*② The date picker opens as a dropdown*

Use the dropdown date picker to specify the date range you want to query.

## 2. Date picker details

### 2.1 Use the preset options

![Preset period options](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/03-presets.png)

*Frequently used periods are provided as presets*

Click a **preset period** at the top of the date picker to automatically select that range. Available options:

- Yesterday
- This week
- This month
- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Select schedule (a configured schedule period)
- Custom (specify start and end dates manually)

Click the option you want to apply that period instantly without picking dates manually.

### 2.2 Pick start and end dates manually

![Start and end date input fields](/images/docs/storeinsight/en/getting-started/_images/date-picker/04-start-end.png)

*Pick start and end dates directly from the calendar*

- **Start date**: The start date on the date picker calendar.
- **End date**: The end date on the date picker calendar.

#### Custom date range

![Start date selection interaction](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/05-start-pick.png)

*Select start date — click the date you want*

![End date selection interaction](/images/docs/storeinsight/en/getting-started/_images/date-picker/06-end-pick.png)

*Select end date — click the final date and the range appears on the calendar*

- Click **Select start date** and pick the date you want.
- Click **Select end date** and pick the final date.
- The selected range is highlighted on the calendar and applies as soon as you click **Apply**.

### 2.3 Change the calendar month and year

![Month and year navigation](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/07-month-year-nav.png)

*Use the dropdowns and arrows above the calendar to change month and year*

You can change the year and month using the dropdowns at the top of the calendar and the left/right arrow buttons.

![Year dropdown](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/08-year-dropdown.png)

*Pick the year you want from the year dropdown*

**Change the year**

- Click the **year dropdown** at the top of the screen to see the list of years available.
- Click the year you want, and the calendar refreshes to that year's months.

**Change the month**

- Click the **month dropdown** to pick a month directly.
- Or use the **◀ / ▶ arrow buttons** to move to the previous/next month.

> **💡 팁: Dropdown vs. arrows**
>
> - **Dropdown**: when you want to jump to a specific year or month in one step
> - **Arrows**: when you want to move month by month

### 2.4 Check operating days

![Operating day indicators](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/09-operating-days.png)

*Operating days are marked with a blue dot (●) under the date*

**Operating day indicator**

- Days that are operating days are marked with a blue dot (●) under the date.
- This makes it easy to see your store's actual operating days at a glance.

**Operating days / queried days**

- After picking a range, the date picker shows the count at the bottom in the format `operating days / queried days`.
- Example: `6 days / 8 days` → 6 of the 8 selected days are actual operating days.

### 2.5 Compare against another period

![Compare toggle](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/10-compare-toggle.png)

*Toggle for comparison analysis*

When you need a comparison analysis, enable **Compare**.

![Selecting query date and comparison date together](/images/docs/storeinsight/getting-started/_images/date-picker/11-compare-set.png)

*Specify both the query date (base period) and the comparison date (period to compare against)*

- Pick a **query date** (base period) and a **comparison date** (the period to compare against) separately.
- View the data for both periods side by side.
- Use the **preset periods** under the comparison section to auto-fill the comparison range.

Available options:

- Preceding period(match day of week)
- Preceding period
- Same period last year(match day of week)
- Same period last year
- Select schedule (a configured schedule period)
- Custom (specify start and end dates manually)
