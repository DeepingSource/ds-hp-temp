This dashboard diagnoses staffing and service efficiency on a single screen. It shows key metrics such as the grade score, counter coverage, shelf management, and the staff-to-customer ratio. Use it to verify the reasoning behind the score and to adjust staffing by time of day.

## Key Features

- **Grade score** — Combines the counter, shelf, and activity scores into an efficiency grade.
- **Key metrics** — Check counter coverage, counter gaps, the staff-to-customer ratio, and more.
- **Verify the score's basis** — Revisit item by item how the grade was calculated.
- **Staffing recommendations** — Pinpoints understaffed time slots and suggests a staffing plan.

## How to Use

1. Ask something like "Analyze staff efficiency," or open the dashboard.
2. Select the date and the start and end times, then run the analysis.
3. Review the grade score and the key metrics.
4. Use the score-basis verification and the staffing analysis to find the time slots to adjust.

## Tips

- For understaffed slots, flexible staffing tends to raise service density better than a fixed single-person assignment.
- Use the score-basis verification to check the lowest items first and set your improvement priorities.
