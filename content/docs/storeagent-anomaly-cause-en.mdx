It detects situations where a specific product's sales suddenly drop or waste increases, and traces back the reasons behind it. Ask naturally, like "Why did this drop so much?", and it queries the sales data to explain the cause of the change.

## Key Features

- **Sales-drop cause analysis** — Compares the recent period with the previous one to show how much sales volume fell and where demand shifted.
- **Waste anomaly detection** — Judges whether there is a problem by comparing yesterday's waste quantity and waste rate against a baseline.
- **Cause & recommended action** — Expand the details to see the trend, the estimated cause, and the next action to take.

## How to Use

1. Ask about the product or waste situation you are curious about in the chat box.
2. Watch the data query and anomaly check unfold in the process steps.
3. Use the key summary to grasp the scale of the problem first.
4. Expand "Details" to check the cause and recommended action.

## Tips

- Even when the waste rate does not exceed the baseline, reviewing the top items once more can reduce losses.
- When the cause is unclear, ask about other metrics from the same period as well to cross-check.
