**StoreCare** is a store operations monitoring service that automatically watches your store's display, cleanliness, operations, and temperature, and alerts you the moment something goes wrong. Owners and managers can check whether the store is running well from their phone, even when they're away.

This guide walks through each screen **exactly as you'd use the StoreCare mobile app**.


- [Getting Started](/en/resources/docs/storecare-login) — Log in, learn the layout, and set up the basics.
- [Real-time](/en/resources/docs/storecare-live) — Your store status at a glance — store status, anomaly detection, store score.
- [Report](/en/resources/docs/storecare-report) — Pick a past date and look back at how your store did.
- [Inbox](/en/resources/docs/storecare-notification) — See all store alerts in one place and jump to the related screen.

>
