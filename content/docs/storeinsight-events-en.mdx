## What it does

- View information for each event period in a report.
- Select two events at the same time to compare engagement and impact across them.

## 1. Open the Event scheduling screen

![Settings button at the bottom-left of the screen](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/01-settings-button.png)

*① Click the Settings button*

Click the **Settings button** at the bottom-left of the screen to open the Settings page.

![Event scheduling in the left menu](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/02-events-menu.png)

*② Click Event scheduling in the left menu*

In the left menu of the Settings page, click the **Event scheduling** button.

## 2. Copy the calendar address from Google Calendar

![Settings button at the top-right of Google Calendar](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/03-calendar-settings.png)

*③ Click the ⚙️ Settings button at the top-right of Google Calendar*

In Google Calendar — where you'll create the event schedule — click the **⚙️ Settings button** at the top-right.

![Pick a calendar from the Settings left menu](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/04-select-calendar.png)

*④ Pick the calendar where event schedules are stored*

In the left menu of the Settings screen, pick **the calendar where event schedules are stored**.

![iCal-format private address](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/05-ical-url.png)

*⑤ Copy the iCal private address from the Integrate calendar menu*

- Open the **Integrate calendar** menu inside the selected calendar.
- Copy the **iCal-format private address** from the Integrate calendar menu.

<details>
<summary>Is there a registration template for events?</summary>

There is no fixed template. You can freely fill in the title and period.

- **Title**: write so the event content is recognizable at a glance.
- **Period**: set to match the event schedule.

![Example of an event registration](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/06-event-format.png)

*Fill in the title and period freely*

</details>

## 3. Paste the calendar address into StoreInsight

![Enter the iCal URL and click Connect](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/events/07-connect-calendar.png)

*⑥ Enter the iCal URL → click Connect*

- On the Event scheduling screen, paste the **iCal-format private address URL** you copied.
- Click the **Connect** button.

> **📌 Note**
>
> Schedule sync runs starting at midnight, so the list won't update immediately.
