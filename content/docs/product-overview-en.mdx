An introduction to what DeepingSource does as a company, and how the **SAAI** product family connects into a single operating flow. This is the first document for anyone evaluating adoption or just getting started.

## What is DeepingSource

DeepingSource is an **Anonymized Spatial AI** company. It anonymizes the people in the CCTV footage already installed in your store in real time, and then performs spatial analysis and operational automation on top of that. Because the original footage is never stored, you can understand what happens in your store without identifying any individual.

- **103 patents** — 66 registered, 37 filed. The foundation of our anonymization and spatial analysis technology.
- **Three technology pillars** — Anonymizer, Spatial AI, and Agentic AI — the three pillars work together to read and operate a space.
- **Cameraless is also possible** — There is also the saai.store product family, which starts from operational data alone, without cameras.

## The SAAI Operating Loop

SAAI is an **operating loop** in which **three modes** interlock into a single flow. It senses what is happening right now, analyzes what has been happening until yesterday, and proposes what to do next — then learning closes the loop and feeds the result back in. This document set is for HQ and administrators; for products aimed at owners running a single store, see the separate section below.

- **01 · Observe · Now · store care** — Watches the present — detecting theft, hygiene, and equipment anomalies across all stores.
- **02 · Analyze · Yesterday · store insight** — Reads what came before — paths, dwell, and conversion. What happened and why.
- **03 · Suggest · Next · store agent** — Proposes what comes next — judging recommendations and ordering with a single engine. The decision to act stays with a person.
- **04 · Learn · Again** — The output becomes the next input. This step has no product — it is what closes the loop.

**Functions are not products.** The twelve functions — `store count` (trade area, foot traffic, capture rate) among them — belong to no single mode and cross all three: the same count runs through care as *now*, through insight as *yesterday*, and through agent as *next*.

## How It Works

Every product operates on the same principle — **erase before you read**.

1. The original CCTV footage is fed in.
2. AI removes personally identifiable information in real time (anonymization).
3. Anonymized data, retaining only behavioral attributes, is stored — the original is not kept.
4. Models are trained on this data, and insights are output in the form of reports and alerts.

The detailed principles will be covered in the "How Anonymization Works" document in the Privacy & Security section.

If you run a single store yourself rather than an HQ or multi-store operation, the two products below may be a better fit.
