## What store agent does

store agent is a **conversational operations copilot** that reads your store data for you and suggests the next move. Ask about sales, visits, inventory, staff, and CCTV data in one place, get answers, and turn them into action right away.

**Ask, and the right screen opens**

Ask in plain language — like "Give me this morning's briefing" or "Recommend a lunchbox order" — and the matching analysis screen opens.

**Traceable evidence, every time**

Every recommendation comes with its reasoning. Check "why this quantity" against tables and data before you decide.

**People make the decisions**

The AI advises; you decide. Register answers you like as a Task or a memo and carry them through to action.

## How this manual is organized

- **Getting Started** — logging in, selecting a store, the overview screen, and how to use AI chat
- **Using Analysis** — chart questions, anomaly detection, ordering, shelf, and staff analysis, and external context
- **Product Manual** — registering Tasks and memos, stopping a response, and frequently asked questions
