The **Report** tab lets you pick a past date and look back at how the store did that day. If Real-time is "now," Report is the retrospective. The layout is almost the same as the Real-time screen, except the reference point changes from "today" to "the selected date."

![Report top](/images/docs/storecare/en/report/_images/overview.png)

*Pick a date on the calendar to see that day's summary*

## Pick a date

Tap a date to view on the weekly calendar at the top. Below the selected date you'll see `Mon DD Weekday` along with how many days ago it was (e.g. `1 day(s) ago`), plus a summary card that sums up that day's status in one sentence.

## That day's anomaly detection · store score

Based on the selected date, [Store Anomaly Detection](/en/resources/docs/storecare-anomalies) (operational/temperature) and [Store Score](/en/resources/docs/storecare-score-inventory) are shown exactly the same way as on the Real-time screen. Tap a card to open that day's detail list.

![Report store score and stock alert area](/images/docs/storecare/en/report/_images/score-inventory.png)

*That day's store score trend and stock alert area*

## Stock Alert Area

Collects display areas that **held a caution/bad status for 6 hours or more** on the selected day. If there are none, it shows "There are no stock alert display areas."

Next, you can see that day's most active [Popular Display Area](/en/resources/docs/storecare-popular-zones).
