It shows how visitors moved over the past several days and which times of day they cluster in. Ask about the trend and the time-of-day pattern together, and it organizes the visit flow in one go.

## Key Features

- **Visitor trend summary** — Summarizes total visitors for the period, the change from the previous week, and the daily average.
- **Time-of-day pattern** — Finds the busy and quiet times of day to map out the flow of the day.
- **Day-by-day detail data** — Check the highest and lowest visit days with a trend chart and a by-date table.

## How to Use

1. Ask about the visit trend or the time-of-day pattern in the chat box.
2. The process steps handle the visit summary and the time-of-day analysis in order.
3. Check the change and the peak time of day in the key summary.
4. Review the figures by date using the chart on the right and the detail-data table.
