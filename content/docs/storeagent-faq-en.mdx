Here are the questions people run into most often while using Store Agent. If you don't find what you're looking for, check the manual pages below for more detail.

## Frequently asked questions

### I can't see any data

First, check that the date range and store are selected correctly. Pick the target store at the top and adjust the date range on the analysis screen, and the data will appear.

### How do I switch stores

Switch to the store you want from the store selector at the top of the screen. The overview and analysis results refresh based on the store you select.

### How do I turn a recommendation into action

Tap "Save as Task" below the answer you like, and it goes into your task list. If you just want it for reference, use "Save as Memo."

### I want to know why a recommendation was made

Recommendations come with their reasoning. Open "Details" or "View rationale" on an answer to see, in tables and figures, which data it was derived from.

### An answer is taking too long

While a response is being generated, the button in the input field turns into a stop button. If it's heading in the wrong direction, stop it and ask again with a more specific question.

### Where are answers saved

Items saved as a Task go into the task list, and items saved as a memo go into the manager memo list, each with its date.
