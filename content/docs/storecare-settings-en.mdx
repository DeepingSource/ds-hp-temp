Tap the **Settings (⚙️)** icon at the top right of the Real-time screen to open Settings.

![Settings screen](/images/docs/storecare/en/getting-started/_images/settings.png)

*Settings — Notifications · Account · Language · Other*

| Group | Item | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Notifications** | Notification Settings | Manage which alerts you receive. |
| **Account** | My Information | View and manage your account information. |
| **Language** | English | Choose the app's display language (한국어 / 日本語 / English). |
| **Other** | Replay tutorial | Replay the screen tutorial shown at first login. |
