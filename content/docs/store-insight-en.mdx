## What store insight delivers


**Efficient operations**

Allocate more resources to popular zones and improve underperforming ones.
**Higher-impact marketing**

Design targeted campaigns based on the zones each gender and age group actually visits.
**Better customer experience**

Reduce shopping fatigue with optimized store layouts and customer paths.
**Data-driven decisions**

Make faster, more accurate calls grounded in real data.


## Guide structure

- **Getting started** — How to log in, navigate the dashboard, use the date picker, and apply data filters
- **Dashboard settings** — Configure lines, zones, event schedules, and ad performance
- **Reports** — User manual for analytics reports (visitor analysis, heatmaps, paths, funnels, purchase conversion, and more)
- **Troubleshooting / FAQ** — Common questions and how to resolve them
