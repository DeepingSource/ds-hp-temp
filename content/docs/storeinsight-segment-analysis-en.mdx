*Segment Comparative demo*


Segment Comparative is a strategic filter that goes beyond average customer behavior to **precisely compare differences between groups**.

It acts as the core filter for an advanced view that breaks Zone interest analysis results down by group. Use it for precision targeting, personalization, and operations focused on your highest-performing customer segments.

### Visitor group definition

- Define visitor groups based on demographic information such as gender and age range.
- Captures the unique behavioral patterns of the demographic segments you choose.
- **Example:** Set your core target audience and extract their distinctive patterns.

### Comparative Zone interest analysis

- Compare the configured segments to identify differences in core performance metrics — visits, Stays, interest — across groups.
- **Examples:**
  - Identify a space that's a Star Zone for women in their 20s but a Question Mark for men in their 40s, and craft differentiated strategies that match each target group's preferences.
  - Compare how much higher your core target group's interest (Stays) is than non-target groups to quantitatively diagnose the marketing-targeting accuracy of that zone.

## Use cases

### Precision target marketing

- Deliver messages only to the "potentially responsive customers," not the broader crowd, to maximize marketing-spend efficiency.
- Choose the most effective message and channel based on each group's preferred zones and Bounce stages.

### Operational efficiency and ROI

- Concentrate resources and staff on the Star Zones and Hidden Gems of your core target segments to maximize operational efficiency and ROI.

### Personalization and experience improvement

- Analyze interest differences across groups to strengthen merchandising — for example, placing age-specific preferred products in tailored locations.
- Quantitatively confirm which visitor groups responded to a campaign, raising the precision of future strategy.

## Advanced tips

### Deep zone-interest comparison strategy

- After applying segment comparisons, analyze the relative position of visits and interest to diagnose each target group's per-zone potential.
- Compare a single segment's pre- and post-promotion zone interest to quantitatively validate the impact of your strategy.

> **💡 Tip: Summary**
>
> Segment Comparative is an advanced feature that moves past **overall averages** and precisely surfaces the **behavioral character of each group**.
> With it, you can focus all marketing, merchandising, and operational strategy on your **most important customer segments** — securing a clear case for **driving the strongest possible performance**.
