When your question was off, or an answer is taking longer than expected, you can stop a response in progress. While a response is being generated, the send button in the input field turns into a stop button.

## Key features

- **Stop response button** — Tap the button in the input field while an answer is being generated to halt it.
- **Stopped-state indicator** — Once stopped, it shows "The user stopped the response."
- **Ask again right away** — After stopping, you can revise your question and ask again.

## How to use

1. Watch the progress while the AI generates an answer.
2. Tap the stop button in the input field.
3. The response halts and a notice appears saying it was stopped.
4. Refine your question and enter it again.

*When you stop a response, "The user stopped the response" appears below the progress step.*

## Tips

If the answer was heading in the wrong direction from the start, it's faster to stop early and make your question more specific than to wait it out. Stopping leaves your earlier conversation intact.
