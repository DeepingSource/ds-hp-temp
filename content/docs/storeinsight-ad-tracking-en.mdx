## What it does

- Designate where ads are placed and where they're viewed from on the 2D map.
- Ad performance analysis runs based on those designated zones.

## Usertool — Display Stand Setting

### 1. Open the line/zone drawing screen

![Line/zone drawing in the left menu](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/01-line-zone-button.png)

*Click the line/zone drawing button in the left menu*

In the left menu, click the **Line/zone drawing button** to open the line/zone drawing screen.

### 2. Pick the ad performance analysis point

![Select camera view](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/02-select-camera.png)

*Pick the camera view that captures the ad you want to analyze*

In the camera views on the left of the screen, click **the camera view that captures the ad you want to analyze**.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> Pick the view that **best shows the customer's gaze (the direction they're looking)**, not just the view where the ad itself is most visible.

### 3. Draw the ad location (Attention Target — AT)

![Activate the zone tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/03-at-tool.png)

*Click the zone tool in the top toolbar*

![Draw the ad area as a polygon (animation)](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/04-at-draw.gif)

*Place multiple points to draw the ad shape as a polygon*

- Click the **zone tool** in the top toolbar to draw a zone wherever you want.
- Place **several points along the ad shape to form a polygon**.
- Right-click or press Enter to finish.

> **⚠️ Caution**
>
> If the same ad is captured from multiple angles, **draw only one of them**.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> To delete a zone, select it and press Delete or Backspace.

### 4. Confirm the ad analysis point

![Set zone name and purpose, then click Complete](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/05-at-confirm.png)

*Enter a name → set purpose to Attention Target (AT) → Complete*

- Set the **zone name**. (e.g., `ad for X-mas`)
- Set the **zone purpose** to `Attention Target (AT)`.
- Click the **Complete button** to lock in the analysis point.

> **📌 Note**
>
> You can edit the analysis point's name and purpose by **right-clicking** the zone.

### 5. Draw the floor area where the ad is visible (Visible Area — VA)

![Click the zone tool](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/06-va-tool.png)

*Click the zone tool again*

![Draw the floor area as a polygon (animation)](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/07-va-draw.gif)

*Draw a polygon on the floor area where customers can see the ad*

- Click the **zone tool** in the top toolbar.
- Draw a **polygon by placing several points** on the floor area where customers can view the ad.
- Right-click or press Enter to finish.

### 6. Confirm the visible area

![Set the visible area's name, purpose, and connected ads](/images/docs/storeinsight/settings/_images/ad-tracking/08-va-confirm.png)

*Set name and purpose (VA) → connect the visible ads → Complete*

- Set the **zone name**. (e.g., `X-mas - Floor3`)
- Set the **zone purpose** to `Visible Area (VA)`.
- **Select every ad visible from this floor area**.
- Click the **Complete button** to lock in the analysis point.

> **📌 Note**
>
> Analysis begins on the day you draw the zone, but the results become available **the following day**.
