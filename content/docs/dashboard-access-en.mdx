This guide explains how to get a dashboard account, log in, and add team members. Permission scopes and data access policies are covered in more detail in the Access Permission Management document.

## Account Structure

Dashboard accounts are issued **per store or per HQ (multi-store)**. As a rule, an HQ account can view reports across multiple stores, while a store account is configured to see only that store's data. The specific configuration may vary depending on the contract and organizational structure discussed at onboarding.

## Getting an Account

During the account issuance step of the onboarding process, the access address (URL) for each store or HQ and the initial administrator account (ID and password) are issued. Any additional accounts needed afterward can be requested through the initial administrator.

## Logging In

1. Go to the access address you were issued.
2. Enter your ID and password.
3. Once you log in, you enter the dashboard for the store (or multiple stores) that matches your permission scope.

## Adding Team Members

If several people at a store or HQ need to use the dashboard together, you can request additional team member accounts through the administrator account you were first issued. You can also set a different scope of viewable stores for each team member — see the Access Permission Management document for detailed permission design.

## Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, contact your administrator or your DeepingSource account manager to request a reset. When you can't log in, first check whether your ID and password are correct and whether Caps Lock is on; if it still doesn't work, clear your browser cache and try again.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between an HQ account and a store account?
As a rule, an HQ account can view reports across multiple stores, while a store account is configured to see only that store's data.

### What happens to accounts when someone leaves or is reassigned?
You can deactivate the account or adjust its access scope through your administrator.

### I don't know the access address.
The access address (URL) and accounts are issued by an administrator for each store or HQ. Contact your administrator.
