**Popular Display Area** sits at the very bottom of the Report screen. It shows how many times empty displays were refilled, as color intensity over the store floor plan.

![Popular display area heatmap](/images/docs/storecare/en/report/_images/popular-zones.png)

*Darker colors mark areas that were emptied and refilled more often*

- **The darker the color**, the more often that area was emptied and refilled that day.
- A high refill count is a signal that **more product moved** (i.e. more visits/sales).
- The color bar below the plan (e.g. `0 time(s)` ~ `3 time(s)`) is the intensity scale.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> Darker areas may be your best-selling spots; lighter areas may get less attention. Use this when rethinking display placement or shelf layout.
