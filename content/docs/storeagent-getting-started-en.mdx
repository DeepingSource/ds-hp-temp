store agent is a store operations copilot you query through conversation. Ask a question in chat and it analyzes your visit, sales, and inventory data and answers on the spot. When you first sign in, the chat start screen appears on the left and the store overview metrics appear on the right, side by side.

## Key features

- **Conversational Q&A** — ask in plain language and it analyzes your store data to answer.
- **Suggested questions** — common questions are offered as buttons, so you can start with a single click.
- **Store overview** — customer and store metrics are always shown on the right side of the screen.
- **Period filter** — switch the data by weekday, weekend, last two weeks, or previous month.

## How to use it

1. After logging in, select your store from the top bar.
2. Click one of the suggested questions under "What would you like to know?" on the left, or type your question directly into the input box at the bottom.
3. Check the store metrics in the overview on the right while you follow up with more questions.

## Tips

- Starting with a suggested question is the quickest way to grasp what kinds of analysis are possible.
- Following up after you get an answer lets you look deeper while keeping the context.
