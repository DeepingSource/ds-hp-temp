A guide to the stages of deployment, from contract to your first report. The exact timeline varies depending on the store environment and the number of CCTV cameras, and your account manager will provide specifics after a pre-check.

## The Overall Flow

Deployment proceeds in six stages: **contract and kickoff → on-site survey and pre-check → installation → account provisioning → dashboard training → first report review**. Because we make use of existing CCTV rather than installing new cameras, the key to preparation is not installing new cameras but accurately understanding your current CCTV and network environment.

## Deployment Stages

1. **Contract and kickoff** — After the contract is signed, an account manager is assigned. Based on the number of stores, industry, and goals, you together finalize the deployment schedule and the required product configuration (store insight, store care, store agent, etc.).
2. **On-site survey and pre-check** — We check the store floor plan, the number, field of view, and installation locations of existing CCTV cameras, and the network environment (internet bandwidth, whether a closed network is used). For items that need to be verified, refer to the Environment Requirements document.
3. **Installation** — Without adding new cameras, we connect anonymized analysis to the CCTV already installed in the store. Depending on the store and network environment, this is done on-site or remotely.
4. **Account provisioning** — Once installation is complete, a per-store dashboard access address (URL) and an administrator account (ID and password) are issued.
5. **Dashboard training** — We run an onboarding session for the relevant staff, guiding them on how to read reports and how to configure the dashboard (lines and zones, event schedules, etc.).
6. **First report review** — After data collection begins, you review your first report. The time required varies depending on the store environment, and detailed guidance will be covered in the Getting Your First Report document.

## Preparation Checklist

Checking the items below in advance, before the pre-check (stage 2), speeds up the schedule. For detailed criteria, refer to the Environment Requirements document.

- **Existing CCTV status** — Organizing the number of CCTV cameras in the store, their installation locations, and their field of view (the coverage of the entrance, shelves, checkout, etc.) makes the pre-check faster.
- **Network environment** — Please check the configuration of the network to which the cameras and server are connected, the internet bandwidth, and whether a closed network is used.
- **Assigned staff** — Designating in advance the store and HQ staff who will coordinate the installation schedule and receive dashboard training keeps things running smoothly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to install new cameras?
No. In most cases, the CCTV already in the store is used as is. Only in special cases where the field of view or number of cameras is insufficient for analysis will we advise on additions or adjustments.

### How long does deployment take?
It varies depending on the number of stores and the existing CCTV and network environment. Your account manager will provide the exact schedule after the pre-check.

### Can I deploy multiple stores at once?
Yes. For multi-store deployment, we coordinate the per-store pre-check and a sequential installation schedule together.
