StoreInsight provides a wide range of reports that help you analyze every stage of the customer journey. The reports available to you may vary depending on your installed product configuration.

> **💡 Tip**
>
> If a report you need is not in this list, please contact Deeping Source.

## Part 1. Understand your store's current state


- [Visitor analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-visitor-analysis) — Comprehensive analysis of visitor count, demographics, and dwell time
- [Line analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-line-analysis) — Customer statistics for virtual line crossings


## Part 2. Rediscover the value of your space


- [Zone analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-zone-analysis) — Visitor and dwell-time analysis by zone
- [Heat map](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-heatmap) — Visualize customer movement
- [Heatmap by time](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-time-heatmap) — See how heatmaps change over time


## Part 3. Track the customer journey


- [Visitor path](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-visitor-trajectory) — Trace individual customer paths
- [Inflow Rate Analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-inflow-rate) — Entry ratio relative to entrance traffic
- [Inter-Zone Traffic](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-inter-zone-traffic) — Analyze movement between zones
- [Main path](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-representative-path) — The path most customers actually follow


## Part 4. Diagnose issues and optimize performance


- [Zone interest analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-zone-interest) — Visits vs. interest
- [Purchase conversion analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-conversion-rate) — Visit-to-purchase ratio


## Part 5. Custom analytics and communication


- [Segment Comparative](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-segment-analysis) — Compare by gender and age group
- [Custom report](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-custom-report) — Build reports with the conditions you need


## Add-ons


- [Funnel Analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-funnel) — Diagnose drop-off at each stage
- [Ad performance](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-ad-viewing) — Viewer count, gaze, and demographics for in-store ads
- [Display Shelf Heatmap](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-shelf-heatmap) — Gaze and dwell at the shelf level
- [Display Shelf Analysis](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-shelf-analysis) — Shelf-level visit, pickup, and dwell data
- [Seat Occupancy Rate](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-seat-occupancy) — Real-time seat usage
- [Event schedule](/en/resources/docs/storeinsight-event-schedule) — Auto-aggregate data across event periods
