Fresh food and lunch boxes easily end up as waste after just a day. Order Recommendations calculates each product's sales flow and waste risk together to suggest the right order quantity. It gathers onto a single screen the basis a purchasing manager needs to decide between waste and stockouts.

## Key Features

- **Recommended order quantity** — Suggests the right quantity for each product by reflecting the 60-day average, forecast sales, and recent trends.
- **Waste and margin metrics** — Displays waste rate, margin, and expected daily profit together to provide the basis for order decisions.
- **Day-of-week adjustment** — Adjusts the order volume by reflecting demand fluctuations such as rain forecasts.
- **KPI summary** — Check the number of products, total order quantity, waste-alert products, and new products at the top.

## How to Use

1. Search for the target by product name or category.
2. Adjust the analysis period to the number of days you want.
3. Turn on day-of-week adjustment if needed.
4. Check the recommended order quantity and reasoning for each product in the recommendation detail table.

## Tips

For products with a high waste rate, the recommended quantity is set conservatively. Lunch box sales tend to drop on rainy days, so reviewing the reduction range with day-of-week adjustment can help reduce over-ordering.
