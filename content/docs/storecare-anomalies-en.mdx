Scroll down the Real-time screen to find the **Store Anomaly Detection** area. It summarizes today's anomalies into two counts — **Operational Anomaly** and **Temperature Anomaly**.

![Store anomaly detection cards](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/anomaly-cards.png)

*Operational and Temperature anomaly counts (tap a card for the detail list)*

> **📌 Note: About the example screens**
>
> The list/detail example images on this page use **demo store data** to show what an anomaly looks like when one occurs. When the store is normal, you'll see the "empty" screens below instead.

## Operational Anomaly

Tap the **Operational Anomaly** card to open the operational anomaly list by date. It collects operations-related situations such as a missing uniform, an unattended register, or an outsider at the register.

![Operational anomaly list](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/ops-list-example.png)

*Example: each item shows a snapshot, type, area, time, and duration*

- Pick another date on the date strip at the top to view that day's records.
- Each item shows an on-site snapshot along with the **type**, **area**, **time**, and **duration**.
- Tap an item to open the detail screen.

![Operational anomaly detail](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/ops-detail-example.png)

*Example: a play bar to scrub through snapshots over time*

On the detail screen you can check the area and type, the start–end time and duration, and replay what the spot looked like with the time-based snapshot play bar.

If there were no operational anomalies today, an empty screen is shown.

![Operational anomaly empty screen](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/ops-empty.png)

*A day with no operational anomalies*

## Temperature Anomaly

Tap the **Temperature Anomaly** card to check the status of refrigeration and freezer units.

![Temperature anomaly list](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/temp-list-example.png)

*Example: Refrigerator/Freezer filters and current temperature/status per unit*

- Use the **Refrigerator / Freezer** filter at the top to narrow down by unit type.
- Each card shows the unit's **current temperature**, name, type, and status (Normal/Danger).
- Tap a card to open the per-unit detail screen.

![Temperature anomaly detail](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/temp-detail-example.png)

*Example: temperature graph over time with danger thresholds*

On the detail screen you can see the min/max temperature, the **danger threshold** (e.g. `0.0°C or below, 10.0°C or above`), and the temperature graph over time (today vs. yesterday). When it crosses the threshold the graph turns red, and "Danger" appears when an out-of-threshold state lasts 30 minutes or more.

If there are no temperature anomalies, an empty screen is shown.

![Temperature anomaly empty screen](/images/docs/storecare/en/live/_images/temp-empty.png)

*No temperature anomalies detected — the store is operating normally*
