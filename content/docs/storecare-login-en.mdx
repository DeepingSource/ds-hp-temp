When you open the StoreCare app, the login screen appears. Enter the **email** and **password** you registered with and tap **Log in**.

![StoreCare login screen](/images/docs/storecare/en/getting-started/_images/login.png)

*Login screen — enter your email and password*

- **Email · Password** — the Log in button activates once both fields are filled. Use the eye icon on the right of the password field to reveal what you typed.
- **Change password** — use this if you forgot your password or want to change it.
- **Sign up** — register here if you don't have an account yet.

> **💡 Tip: First-time login**
>
> The first time you log in, a short step-by-step tutorial highlights the main screens. You can **Skip** it, and replay it later from [Settings](/en/resources/docs/storecare-settings) → **Replay tutorial**.
