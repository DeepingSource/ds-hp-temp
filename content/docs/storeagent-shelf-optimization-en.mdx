Even the same product sells differently depending on where it is placed. Shelf Rearrangement Optimization suggests rearrangement strategies based on expected additional sales and compares before and after through simulation. Shelf layouts once moved by intuition can now be reviewed with data.

## Key Features

- **Rearrangement strategy list** — Presents multiple strategies in order of expected additional sales (e.g., Shelf Efficiency Optimization +₩440,300).
- **Pair selection** — Select a pair of products to swap positions and designate them as the experiment target.
- **Before/After simulation** — Compares the customer flow and results before and after applying the selected pair.
- **Expected additional sales** — Calculates the monthly expected additional sales, including conservative and optimistic ranges.

## How to Use

1. Select the rearrangement strategy you want from the strategy list.
2. Select the product pair to swap.
3. Run the simulation to check the before-and-after results.
4. If the results are satisfactory, apply them by rearranging in person.

## Tips

Review strategies starting with the largest expected additional sales, but checking the conservative and optimistic ranges together makes your judgment more stable. Confirm the effect through simulation, then apply it by rearranging in person.
