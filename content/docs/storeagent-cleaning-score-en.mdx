This feature looks up the trend of your store's cleaning score. Ask something like "How has the cleaning score been trending lately?" and you can see the recent rise and fall over the period. Use it to check your hygiene management status on a regular basis.

## Key Features

- **Score trend lookup** — Check the cleaning score trend over the recent period.
- **Honest notice** — When there's no data, it doesn't make anything up; it tells you plainly that the lookup returned nothing.
- **Alternatives offered** — When a lookup fails, it suggests substitute indicators such as quality trends and at-risk products.

## How to Use

1. Ask in the chat window, for example: "How has the cleaning score been trending lately?"
2. If the lookup succeeds, review the recent trend and the key summary.
3. If it returns nothing, follow the notice and check the substitute indicators.
4. Register any needed action as a Task or a note.

## Tips

- When there's no data, the store agent doesn't invent a number; it honestly tells you the lookup returned nothing. That makes it a more trustworthy basis for judgment than a groundless answer.
- If the lookup fails, use the quality trend and at-risk product indicators the notice suggests to check instead.
