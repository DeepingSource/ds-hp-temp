- Analyzes the share of customers who passed through specific zones or lines in your store to gauge **zone-level interest and path efficiency**.
- Goes beyond raw visitor counts to show the **share of actual entrants relative to entrance traffic**, helping you prioritize marketing, merchandising, and operations.


## Report layout

![Inflow Rate Analysis view](/images/docs/storeinsight/reports/customer-journey/_images/inflow-rate/01.png)

*Inflow Rate Analysis showing entry share relative to external foot traffic*


The Inflow Rate Analysis report analyzes the **share of actual visitors relative to the external foot traffic in front of the store**.

For F&B stores like Tous Les Jours and Lotteria, it shows the total foot traffic passing the storefront and the percentage that entered. This is distinct from the entry rate into a specific zone in retail stores like Olive Young.

## Analytical view

- **Understand external foot traffic characteristics:** Measure the volume, direction, and gender / age mix of people walking past the store.
- **Inflow path analysis:** Identify the directions visitors enter from.
- **Non-entrant analysis:** Inspect the demographics of people who walked past but didn't enter, and apply the findings to commercial-area strategy.

## Features and calculation

- **Inflow rate (%)** = (people passing the store entrance ÷ people passing in front of the store) × 100
- Optional staff exclusion and gender / age filters
- Compare inflow rate trends by time of day and day of week

## External traffic line data

The **external traffic line** displayed at the bottom of the report shows the foot traffic and customer characteristics of the commercial area in front of your store. This lets you objectively analyze the **commercial scale and flow of that location**, regardless of inflow.

## Use cases

- **Commercial-area attractiveness assessment** — Investigate entry barriers when the storefront has heavy foot traffic but a low inflow rate.
- **Promotion impact** — Compare inflow rates before and after an event.
- **Site strategy refinement** — Adjust signage and display placement to reflect direction- and time-based inflow differences.
- **VMD prioritization** — Reassess displays and POP for zones near the entrance that show low inflow.
- **Ad ROI** — Measure the actual entry rate into zones an ad campaign was meant to drive traffic to.
