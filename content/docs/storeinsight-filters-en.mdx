A range of filters lets you view only the data that matches specific criteria.

## Metric

- **Visitor**: distinct visitors with duplicates removed
- **Visits**: total visits, including duplicates

## Property

- Customer / staff
- Male / female
- By age group

## Age group configuration

The default age group bands (e.g., 10-year intervals) can be freely adjusted to 5-year, 20-year, or other intervals based on your request. Contact Deeping Source if you need a change.
