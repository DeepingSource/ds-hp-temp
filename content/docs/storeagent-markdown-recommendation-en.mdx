Products nearing expiration or the end of season lead to waste if left as they are. Markdown Recommendations distinguishes between products to move to discount selling and products better handled by adjusting order volume. Based on expected waste losses, it organizes what to handle first.

## Key Features

- **Markdown candidate selection** — Selects products to discount immediately based on waste rate and recent sales flow.
- **Discount vs. turnover adjustment** — Distinguishes products better suited to discounting from those better suited to reducing order volume.
- **Expected waste loss** — Shows the expected loss amount per product along with the total loss scale.
- **Discount and placement suggestions** — Suggests execution methods such as the discount rate and whether to place products at the front.

## How to Use

1. Request recommendations for products that need a markdown.
2. Check the items to proceed with immediately and the ones to hold in the key summary.
3. Review each product's waste rate, recent sales, and inventory in the detailed figures.
4. Execute with the suggested discount rate and placement.

## Tips

Even if the waste rate is high, a product whose recent sales have risen sharply may be better handled by adjusting orders than by discounting. Check the detailed figures and execute by separating discount targets from turnover-adjustment targets.
