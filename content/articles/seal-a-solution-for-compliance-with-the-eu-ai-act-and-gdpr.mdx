The EU AI Act mandates high transparency for datasets and algorithms used in AI system training, including clear documentation of data sources and processing methods. Deeping Source's SEAL service is an innovative solution designed to meet these requirements while offering additional benefits.

‍

### Key Features and Benefits of SEAL Service

1.  **Removal of Personal Identifiable Information (PII) While Maintaining Data Utility**:SEAL effectively removes personal identifiable information such as faces and license plates, yet preserves the critical data necessary for AI training. This ensures that AI models maintain their accuracy while adhering to privacy protection standards. This capability allows companies to uphold AI model performance and comply with privacy regulations simultaneously.
2.  **Compliance with GDPR and Other Privacy Regulations**:SEAL automatically anonymizes personal data to comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. This minimizes legal risks for companies by ensuring that data is collected and processed in a compliant manner from the outset. With data anonymized upon collection, there is no need for additional steps to meet regulatory requirements.
3.  **Easier Compliance for High-Risk AI Systems**:According to the EU AI Act, high-risk AI systems are subject to more stringent regulations. SEAL facilitates compliance by removing personal identifiable information from the datasets used by these systems. This makes it easier to meet the stringent requirements set for high-risk applications, helping organizations avoid legal and regulatory complications.
4.  **Real-Time Data Anonymization**:SEAL can be deployed on edge devices, enabling real-time anonymization of data at the point of collection. This eliminates the need for separate anonymization processes post-collection, ensuring that data is immediately compliant with regulations. Real-time anonymization reduces the risk of data breaches and enhances the efficiency of data processing, supporting the rapid development of AI systems.
