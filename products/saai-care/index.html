Every store watched to the same standard — from one screen.

saai care puts the same trained eye on theft, hygiene, and equipment in every location — and rolls every alert up to headquarters. No raw footage, ever.

01

Loss prevention, standardized

Theft and after-hours anomalies caught by one consistent standard in every store — not the judgment of whoever's on shift. Patterns that repeat across locations surface on the HQ screen, so you act on the trend, not just the incident.

02

Hygiene & equipment, on the record

Fridge temperatures, floor spills, blocked exits, doors left open — checked around the clock and time-stamped. Turn "we're pretty sure it's fine" into an audit trail you can show.

03

The whole fleet, one screen

Like saai count and saai insight, saai care rolls up to HQ. Rank stores by incidents, see which sites need a visit, and step in before a small lapse becomes a recall.

Anonymized at the point of capture by SEAL. No footage is stored and identities are erased on the spot — only the events are kept. Compliance you can defend, without trading away privacy to get it.

    How saai care puts all twelve functions to work

    Functions belong to no single product. What this product IS, is the time axis and purpose this mode reads them through.

    • store countFootfall & occupancy

      Live occupancy and crowding, with alerts when a threshold is crossed

    • store censusAnonymous demographics

      Who is in the store right now — estimated age band and gender mix

    • store trailMovement paths

      Unusual congestion or abnormal paths in a specific zone

    • store gazeAttention · gaze→pick

      Live reaction to a new display or POP

    • store waitQueues & waiting

      Real-time alert when a checkout queue crosses its threshold

    • store tideCrowding (ebb & flow)

      Store-wide crowding sensed as it ebbs and flows

    • store keepLoss prevention

      Live detection of abnormal behavior, walk-outs and shrink risk

    • store shelfShelf & stock condition

      Out-of-stock, disarray and price-tag errors, detected live

    • store motionBehavior

      Live detection of incidents — falls, altercations and the like

    • store fitTrend fit

      Early warning when a new product reacts unexpectedly

    • store popIn-store promotion (POP)

      Whether posted POP is visible and intact

    • store talkMultilingual service

      Live detection of foreign-language and help-needed moments
