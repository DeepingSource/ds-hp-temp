Who · What

It doesn’t stop at seeing — it operates.

DeepingSource reads offline spaces safely with anonymized AI. saai suite is one operating loop that analyzes yesterday (insight), detects the now (care), and acts on next (agent). We keep no footage, and the decision is always a person’s.

Key terms, defined

Store Heatmap

Spatial data that shows where customers lingered, visualized in color

Dwell Time Analysis

A metric that gauges interest by how long customers stay in a given space

Purchase Conversion Rate

The share of visitors who actually buy — a core measure of store efficiency

Store Operations Automation

An operating model in which AI automatically handles repetitive store checks and responses

Store AI Agent

An execution AI that turns analytics results into on-site action

Retail AI
