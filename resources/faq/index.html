Function · crosses all three modes

store count

It counts both the people passing your storefront and the ones who actually come in, reading the trade area and location through the capture rate. You can also see gender, age group, and footfall by time of day.

You start with just an AI box and a mobile setup. You don't need new cameras — any spot that can see your storefront works, and setup takes only a few minutes.

Footage is analyzed only after anonymization and deleted immediately. No faces or identities are kept — only statistics are stored — so counting is safe even without individual consent.

You get a funnel from footfall → entries → capture rate, plus gender, age, and time-of-day trends as a report. You can download it straight to Excel for HQ reporting or store-to-store comparison.

You can keep using it after opening, too — tracking how the trade area and footfall shift over time. This footfall data flows together with saai insight (yesterday), care (now), and agent (next).
