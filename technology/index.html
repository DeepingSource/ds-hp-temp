Autonomous Store

From L0 to L5 — one rung at a time

It is not automatic from day one. Per category, you climb the ladder of recommend → approve → automate → verify → learn one rung at a time. But 'decision and accountability' stay with people to the end.

  1. Observe only

    People make every decision (legacy dashboard)

  2. Inform

    Context alerts (weather · season · events)

  3. Recommend priority

    Top 1–3 + reasons (patrol route · display tidy-up)

  4. [Approve] action

    Concrete action + simulation (order tuning · rosters)

  5. Low-risk auto + review

    Repetitive, low-risk automation (alert thresholds · recurring orders)

  6. Autonomous store

    People handle only strategy, accountability, hospitality

Condition to climb — owner cumulative approval rate ≥ 80%, ≥ 30 verified outcomes, explicit HQ–owner agreement. On conflict, roll back one rung immediately.
