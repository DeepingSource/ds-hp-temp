01 · Spatial AI

Not who, but what and how

Vision AI ultimately looks at people. Spatial intelligence changes what it looks for: instead of singling out who someone is, it reads what people do and how they move. Identity is already erased at the anonymization stage — all that remains is the flow of the space.

02 · Vision Models

State-of-the-art vision models

Person detection, pose estimation, re-identification, crowd density — a stack of SOTA vision models reads all of this from a single feed. Where people pause, how they move, how densely they gather. Everything happening on the floor, read on the cameras already mounted in your store.

03 · MTMC

MTMC — scattered cameras, one space

A single camera only ever sees a sliver of the space. MTMC (Multi-Target Multi-Camera) registers scattered cameras into one spatial coordinate system. When the camera changes, the person stays one person — tracked across blind spots, without a break.

04 · Output

Into one spatial coordinate

Once observations from many cameras converge into one coordinate system, you get the full path from entry to exit, plus space-level dwell, density, and movement patterns. The first step toward AI that goes beyond seeing to operate the space — reading it precisely.
